What the Lab Does

We design practical sensing and AI systems that work in real life, not just in the lab. Our work blends ubiquitous computing, machine learning, and human factors to improve health and wellbeing.

  • Human‑centered AI: interpretable, privacy‑aware models for mobile and wearable sensing.
  • Fair & robust ML: methods that handle natural distribution shifts and personalize responsibly.
  • Edge & CPS: efficient, reliable models for resource‑constrained devices and deployments.
  • Application areas: stress & affect, speech and motor disorders, chronic conditions, and population health.
